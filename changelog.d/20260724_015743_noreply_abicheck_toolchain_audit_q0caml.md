### Fixed

- **C++20 detection now restricts the `#if __cplusplus < N` inverted
  polarity to the actual C++20 boundary (`202002L`/`201703L`).** The
  previous fix inverted *every* less-than comparison uniformly, but a
  check against an older standard's threshold (`__cplusplus < 201103L`,
  "before C++11") isn't about the C++20 decision at all — there the
  `#else` arm is practically always active, and blanket-inverting wrongly
  masked it, hiding a genuine, unconditional C++20 construct there.
