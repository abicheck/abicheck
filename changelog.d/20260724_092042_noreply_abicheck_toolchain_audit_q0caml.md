### Fixed

- **C++20 auto-detection now recognizes a project-defined concept's
  constrained placeholder return type even with a leading decl-specifier
  keyword or attribute.** `inline MyConcept auto f();` and `[[nodiscard]]
  MyConcept auto f();` (and any combination of the two, in either order)
  left the text immediately before the concept name ending mid-keyword
  or mid-attribute instead of a recognized statement boundary, so the
  return-type form went undetected and the header was parsed without
  `-std=gnu++20`.
