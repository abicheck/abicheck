### Fixed

- **C++20 detection now recognizes a project-defined concept's
  constrained parameter or return type even with a leading cv-qualifier**
  (`void f(const MyConcept auto& x);`, `const MyConcept auto f();`). The
  boundary check previously saw `const`/`volatile` where it expected the
  enclosing `(`/`,` or a statement boundary and rejected the construct
  outright, so a header whose only C++20 signal used this cv-qualified
  form went undetected and was parsed without `-std=gnu++20`.
