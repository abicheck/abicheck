### Fixed

- **C++20 detection now treats `#if defined(__cplusplus)`/`#ifdef
  __cplusplus`/bare `#if __cplusplus` as settling their chain, like `#if
  1`/`#if true` already do.** Previously these fell through as
  "unrecognized," leaving their `#else` (C-only fallback) arm scanned
  right alongside the active arm — so a dual C/C++ header's `#else`
  compatibility shim (e.g. `struct concept {};`) stayed visible to the
  shadow-name scan and wrongly shadowed a genuine C++20 declaration in
  the *active* `#if` arm, keeping the header off `-std=gnu++20`.
