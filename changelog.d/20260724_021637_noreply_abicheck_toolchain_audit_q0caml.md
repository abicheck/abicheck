### Fixed

- **C++20 detection now blanks a backslash-continued string literal fully
  in the shadow-name scan, not just in the requirements scan.** Text
  trapped inside such a literal (e.g. an error message reading
  ``"struct concept {}; \`` continued onto the next line) previously
  survived the shadow scan's line-bounded literal stripper, made
  `concept`/`requires`/`consteval`/`constinit` look shadowed as an
  ordinary type name, and suppressed a genuine C++20 declaration
  elsewhere in the header — so castxml/clang was invoked without
  `-std=gnu++20` and rejected the header.
